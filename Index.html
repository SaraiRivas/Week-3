List of Post: The Legend Of Zelda

In this section, you can see the ranking list of the best Legend of Zelda games.

  • 1. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

    There isn't a single puzzle, event, or combat to solve in tons of different ways, after countless hours running through this new version of Hyrule it's clear: The sequel Tears Of The Kingdom is the new best game ever.

  • 2. The Lenged of Zelda: Ocarina Of Time

    The game's story focuses on the young hero Link, who sets out on an adventure in the kingdom of Hyrule to stop Ganondorf, king of the Gerudo tribe, before he finds the Triforce, a sacred relic capable of granting any wish to its possessor.

  • 3. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

    Link, who awakens in a post-apocalyptic world after a hundred years of sleep to defeat Ganon and save the kingdom of Hyrule. Unlike the other predecessor titles in the series, the game features an open world that allows the player to find different ways to complete an objective and the story can be completed in a non-linear fashion.

  • 4. The Lenged of Zelda: A Link to the Past

    Within the official timeline is the first game in the (Hero is Defeated) timeline which connects to an alternate reality scenario in which the Hero of Time is unsuccessful in Ocarina of Time. This results in Ganon being imprisoned in the Sacred Realm in his Dark Beast form out of desperation. Having successfully collected the three pieces of the Triforce, Ganon's evil desires have transformed the realm into the Dark Kingdom.

  • 5. The Lenged of Zelda: Link's Awakening

    Because of a terrible storm, Link is shipwrecked and washed ashore on a strange tropical island inhabited by curious locals. On his adventure to awaken the Wind Fish, Link must travel to different dungeons scattered throughout the island. Travel through each room of these labyrinths as you solve complex puzzles, avoid traps and confront the terrible monsters that await you there.

  • 6. The Lenged of Zelda: Majora's Mask

    At the beginning of the game you will see how a great misfortune looms over the world you inhabit and you only have three short days to stop it. The time runs inexorably and you must manage to complete four great temples to achieve your goal, how can you do it? You will have the help of your ocarina and magical melodies that will allow you to travel back in time to achieve your goals before the final hour arrives.

  • 7. The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess

    The story focuses on Link trying to prevent Hyrule from being engulfed by a parallel dimension called the Twilight Realm. To do so, he takes the forms of both a human and a wolf. He is also assisted by a mysterious creature named Midna. It is supposed to take place after the events of The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask, following the timeline created after the events of The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time.

  • 8. The Lenged of Zelda: The Wind Waker

    Link's quiet life on a small island is altered one fateful day when his beloved sister is kidnapped by a huge bird. Naturally, our hero begins his rescue, sword and shield in hand. The quest leads to an epic adventure on land and sea as Link tries to find clues to his sister's whereabouts.

  • 9. The Lenged of Zelda: Oracles of Ages

    A title in which we will have to go through up to eight dungeons to collect eight essences that allow the Maku tree to bring order to the chaos that the world is living, with the seasons totally crazed

  • 10. The Lenged of Zelda: Skyward Sword

    Descend to the dangerous surface world in this adventure through the skies. During your mission you will explore dangerous dungeons full of puzzles. Each item you obtain will give you access to more discoveries (find a hidden area with the flying beetle or discover ingenious ways to hinder enemies with the whip). Whether you are searching for secrets, practicing swordplay or improving your equipment, in the game The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword you will be able to forge your own destiny.

  • The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap

    Legend that revolves around the Quadruple Sword, the other games being The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords and The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventures. The game is a prequel to Four Swords and reveals some information about the story of the collective games. In the game, Link is accompanied by a talking hat, named Ezero, who will help him throughout his adventure. Also notable is the presence of the Minish race, this being their only appearance in the saga.
